# PROJECT OVERVIEW: ZENDORIA (2D PIXEL ADVENTURE RPG)

## 1. The Goal & Vibe
**The Goal:** Build a fully animated 2D pixel-art adventure RPG set in the cosmic-fantasy world of Zendoria. The game will eventually feature a rich quest system, discoverable gear, and deep exploration.
**The Vibe:** Classic top-down or slightly isometric 2D pixel adventure. Think exploration and questing in the spirit of Zelda or Secret of Mana, mixed with the quirky danger of Pokemon or Earthbound. The world should feel vast, mysterious, and slightly unstable.
**Art Style:** 2D pixel art with smooth sprite animation. The starting island should feel vibrant, hand-crafted, and a little uncanny because reality is flawed there. Glowing flora, warped stone, and cosmic shimmer are all on-tone.

## 2. Core Lore Context (Zendoria Mechanics)
* **The Wormps:** The primary creatures and enemies of this universe. They possess elemental powers and strange anatomies, often including void-like or impossible mouths.
  * *Starter Enemies:* Minor Wormp variants that are easy to animate and fight in the early prototype.
* **The World:** Reality in Zendoria is not stable. Ancient factions, including the Zen'Korah, and legendary figures such as the Four Masters shaped the world.
* **Weapons & Quests:** Players find unique weapons, often with elemental or cosmic origins. Quests should come from curious townsfolk, wanderers, shrines, or environmental discoveries.

## 3. Current Scope: The Starting Island Prototype
While the game should eventually become massive, **we are currently ONLY focusing on the foundation.** The immediate goal is to make the Starting Island feel like a real playable opening slice.

**Mechanics to Build First:**
* **2D Player Controller:** Smooth 4-direction or 8-direction movement.
* **Pixel Animations:** Hook up sprite sheets for idle, walk, and a basic weapon swing.
* **Basic Combat & Weapons:** Simple melee hitbox system with readable feedback.
* **Enemy AI:** A basic wandering/chasing starter Wormp with hit and death responses.
* **The Starting Island Environment:** Tilemap or tile-like authored environment with grass, warped rocks, water boundaries, and a few landmarks.
